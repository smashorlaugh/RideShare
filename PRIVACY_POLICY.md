# Privacy Policy for RideShare

**Last Updated: February 4, 2025**

RideShare ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our mobile application.

### 1. Information We Collect
*   **Phone Number**: We collect your phone number for authentication and to facilitate communication between drivers and riders.
*   **Location Data**: We collect precise location data to help you search for rides, find pickup points, and provide real-time updates during a trip. 
*   **Profile Data**: If provided, we collect your name, photo, and car details to build trust within the platform.
*   **Ride History**: We store details of rides offered and booked to help you manage your trips.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
*   To create and manage your account.
*   To connect riders with available drivers nearby.
*   To enable in-app chat functionality.
*   To allow users to rate and review each other to ensure community safety.

### 3. Data Sharing
We share your name and phone number only with the user you are currently sharing a ride with. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

### 4. Data Security & Storage
Your data is stored securely on our encrypted servers. You can request to delete your account and all associated data at any time via the "Delete Account" button in the app settings.
